Chocolate Chip Cookies

The good old classic: chocolate chip cookies.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Beat together butter, brown sugar and granulated sugar in a large mixing bowl, with an electric mixer, until light and fluffy
  3. Beat in eggs and vanilla.
  4. Add flour mixture gradually to butter mixture; beat until well blended. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts. Drop by rounded tablespoons onto parchment-lined cookie sheets.
  5. Bake 10 to 12 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool.
