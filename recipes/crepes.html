Crepes

An essential recipe for breakfast or brunches!

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 1 1/3 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 pinch of salt

Directions

  1. In large bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, melted butter, flour sugar and salt until smooth.
  2. Heat a medium-sized skillet or crepe pan over medium heat. Grease pan with a small amount of butter.
  3. Spoon about 3 tablespoons crepe batter into hot pan.
  4. Cook over medium heat, 1 to 2 minutes on a side, or until golden brown.
